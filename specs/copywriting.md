# Copywriting Spec — Kaizen IQ

This file defines written voice and approved copy. `CLAUDE.md` remains canonical.

---

## Voice

Kaizen IQ copy should be precise, composed, and concrete. It should sound like a serious partner for data systems, AI capability, and strategic growth.

Rules:
- Short sentences.
- One idea per sentence.
- Concrete before abstract.
- Lead with what Kaizen IQ does, then why it matters.
- Use "Kaizen" deliberately as a philosophy, not as decoration.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid filler.

---

## Approved Vocabulary

Use with restraint:
- compound
- architecture
- leverage
- foresight
- precision
- deliberate
- foundation
- clarity
- permanence

---

## Banned Vocabulary

Do not use:
- unlock
- empower
- revolutionize
- seamless
- robust
- cutting-edge
- world-class
- next-generation
- synergy

---

## Approved Copy

Do not rewrite approved copy without explicit permission.

Hero headline:
```text
The Next Advantage.
```

Hero subhead:
```text
Intelligence compounded over time
```

Hero body:
```text
Data is no longer exhaust — it's leverage. Kaizen IQ partners with leaders to design data systems, AI capabilities, and growth strategies that shape the future of their business. Complexity becomes clarity. Ambition becomes results.
```

Primary CTA:
```text
Begin the conversation
```

Secondary CTA:
```text
Our solutions
```

Hero kana annotation:
```text
改善 — continuous improvement
```

CTA kana annotation:
```text
前進 — advance without limits
```

Solutions page H1:


Solutions subhead:

Contact CTA:

## Metadata Guidance

Meta titles should be concise and brand-forward.

Preferred title patterns:
- `Kaizen IQ — The Next Advantage`
- `Solutions — Kaizen IQ`
- `Contact — Kaizen IQ`

Meta descriptions should state the work plainly. Avoid inflated claims.

Example:
```text
Kaizen IQ designs data systems, AI capabilities, and growth strategies for leaders who need clarity, precision, and durable advantage.
```
