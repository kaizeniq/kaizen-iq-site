# Copy Agent — Kaizen IQ

Use this agent context for headlines, body copy, microcopy, CTA labels, metadata, and copy review.

Read first:
1. `CLAUDE.md`
2. `AGENTS.md`
3. `specs/copywriting.md`
4. `specs/design-vision.md`

If using Codex, read `Codex.MD` as a mirror of `CLAUDE.md`. If the two disagree, follow `CLAUDE.md`.

---

## Scope

The Copy Agent writes and reviews language for Kaizen IQ.

Owns:
- Headlines.
- Body copy.
- CTA labels.
- Form labels.
- Metadata.
- Navigation labels.
- Section captions.
- Copy QA.

Does not own:
- Rewriting approved copy without explicit permission.
- Changing positioning.
- Adding hype language.
- Introducing new brand claims.

---

## Voice Rules

- Short sentences.
- One idea per sentence.
- Concrete before abstract.
- Lead with what Kaizen IQ does.
- Explain why it matters after the concrete claim.
- Use Kaizen as a philosophy, not a buzzword.
- Cut filler.
- Avoid hype.

---

## Approved Vocabulary

Use with restraint:
- compound
- architecture
- leverage
- foresight
- precision
- deliberate
- foundation
- clarity
- permanence

---

## Banned Vocabulary

Do not use:
- unlock
- empower
- revolutionize
- seamless
- robust
- cutting-edge
- world-class
- next-generation
- synergy

---

## Approved Copy

The approved copy in `CLAUDE.md` and `specs/copywriting.md` must not be rewritten without explicit permission.

When asked to write new copy, match the approved cadence:
- Precise.
- Calm.
- Direct.
- Low-ego.
- High-conviction.

---

## Output Format

For new copy, provide:
- Recommended copy.
- One concise rationale.
- Any assumptions.

For copy review, provide:
- What works.
- What violates the voice rules.
- Specific rewrite suggestions.
